FAQ

How do you connect the app to the Hermes Controller hardware?

Ther Hermes Controller automatically connects to the HERMES application upon launch of the app. If you are unable to connect, try one of the following:

  • Verify the Hermes Controller hardware is connected to power and turned on.
  • Ensure the Hermes Controller hardware is within 100 feet of your mobile device.
  • If the device is in range and powered on but unable to connect, a hardware issue is likely affecting your device. Contact support@vitaMotu.com for additional support.

What is the difference between Live, Time Lapse, and Stop Motion modes?

The Hermes Controller offers three modes of operation. The list below details the functionality of each mode:

  • Live Mode
    • Allow direct control of camera position via Left/Right buttons.
    • Provides max speed and damping sliders to control the camera motion profile.
  • Time Lapse Mode
    • Preprogrammed mode allowing continuous motion during video capture.
    • Can be used as an automated second shooter or to create stunning Time Lapse video.
    • Maxium duration of 4 hours 59 minutes or as specified by camera manufacturer
    • NOTE: See camera specifications for maximum shot duration.
  • Stop Motion Mode
    • Preprogrammed mode allowingdiscrete motion during still image capture.
    • Camera moves in discrete steps, capturing still images at each step along the track.
    • Still images are concatinated into video to produce captivating high-speed motion.

What does the "damping" parameter effect?

The Damping parameter adjusts how quickly or slowly the camera reaches maximum speed from at rest. Think of it as the camera's acceleration
